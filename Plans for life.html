My dream job

One of the things I plan to do in life is to do is being the creative director of my animated franchise. The reason I wish to go on that career path is that I’ve always been fascinated with fictional media like video games, anime, and western cartoons. This fascination inspired me to make one of my very own cartoons for the fun of it. But as I grew older, I’m starting to think that I shouldn’t just work in the cartoon industry because I wish to, but because I had to. I feel like animation (mainly adult animation) is stagnating right now. In the past, America used to be the king of pop culture when it comes to movies, games, and cartoons with Japan being it's rival in those industries. But due to animation being viewed as an entire genre by the west rather the medium it is, western animation has started to stagnate depending on the area you're looking at. This is most notable with adult animation as the Simpsons and Family which were once revolutionary cartoons have now flooded the adult animation market with nothing more than generic animated sitcoms that try to edgy and hilarious which they fail to do so. Since I feel like I’m the one person to bring animation back on its feet.
